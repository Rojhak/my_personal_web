Numbers with Heart:
My Journey from Actuarial Tables
to Social Impact.

I've always seen data as more than numbers—it's a lens through which I understand the world, from analyzing client cases to building innovative solutions. From building e-commerce dashboards to fine-tuning NLP models for parliamentary analysis, I've tackled diverse challenges that combine technical expertise with creative problem-solving.

For me, data is a bridge between insight and action—a way to empower decisions and drive meaningful impact. If your team values curiosity, expertise, and a commitment to making a difference, let's work together.
